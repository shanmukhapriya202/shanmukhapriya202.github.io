About Me
Product thinker.
Data-driven doer.

I'm a Product Manager with 5+ years of experience leading SaaS and FinTech product delivery — from first customer conversation to production deployment. I care deeply about translating messy, real-world customer needs into clear requirements that engineering teams can actually build.

My approach combines structured product thinking (roadmaps, user stories, acceptance criteria) with a sharp eye for data — I live in KPI dashboards and usage trends, using them to make decisions that stick.

More recently, I've been exploring how Generative AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft Copilot) can genuinely improve product workflows — not just as novelty, but as practical levers for documentation quality, requirement clarity, and faster iteration.

Based in Hyderabad, pursuing my MBA at Manipal University Jaipur alongside full-time product work.
